About Me

I'm a curious and empathetic UX designer with a strong foundation in user research and a passion for creating meaningful digital products. With over five years of experience, I've worked on projects ranging from mobile apps and enterprise dashboards to e-commerce platforms. My design process is collaborative and data-driven, always putting the user at the center. I believe that good design solves problems and makes people's lives easier.

In my free time, you'll find me sketching, exploring new coffee shops, and trying to master the art of sourdough bread. I'm always eager to learn and take on new challenges.
